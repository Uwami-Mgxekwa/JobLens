What JobLens Does

JobLens is an intelligent job matching platform that connects job seekers with opportunities perfectly suited to their skills, interests, and preferences. We eliminate the noise and help you focus on jobs that truly matter to you.

How It Works

1

Tell Us About Yourself

Complete our simple questionnaire about your skills, interests, location preferences, salary expectations, and work type.

2

We Analyze Your Profile

Our smart matching algorithm compares your profile against available job listings to calculate compatibility scores.

3

Get Personalized Results

View jobs ranked by match percentage, with detailed information to help you make informed decisions.

Our Matching Algorithm

JobLens uses a comprehensive scoring system that evaluates multiple factors:

  • Skills Match: How well your skills align with job requirements
  • Interest Alignment: Compatibility with your industry interests
  • Location Fit: Matches your location and remote work preferences
  • Salary Range: Falls within your expected compensation
  • Work Type: Matches your preferred work environment
